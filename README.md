# Disenchanter Book
Use a Disenchanter Book to remove some enchantments from your tools, like curses or weak enchantments.

# How Get:

**1°** - You need to obtain the **Dark Essence** by killing a wither skeleton with 30% chance.

![Get Dark Essence](doc/1.0%20-%20Get%20Dark%20Essence.gif)

**2°** - You craft the **Disenchanter Book** with 1 dark essence and 1 normal book.

![Craft Disenchanter Book](doc/2.0%20-%20Craft%20Disenchanter%20Book.png)

# How Use:
**1°** - Place the Disenchanter Book in your off hand and the enchanted tool in main hand.

![How Use](doc/3.0%20Show%20How%20Use.png)

**2°** - Use the item (right button or press the screen) to open the disenchant screen.

![Enchantments List](doc/4.0%20-%20Enchantments%20List.png)

**3°** - Select the enchantments you want to remove and click in the **"Disenchant"** button, you will receive a enchanted book with the selected enchantments.

![Result](doc/5.0%20-%20Result.png)
